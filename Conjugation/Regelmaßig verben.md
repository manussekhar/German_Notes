*verb's second part = x
ich -> x+e
du -> x+st
er/sie/es (3rd person)-> x+t 
wir ->  same as verb 
ihr -> x+t 
Sie/sie -> same as verb *

Variation 1
------------------
*if first word in verb ends in t or d*

*du -> x+ est
er/ sie / es / ihr -> x+ et*

Variation 2
------------------
*if first word ends in ß, z, s
du / er /sie / es /ihr -> remove s from st*
